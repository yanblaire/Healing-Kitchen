Visit our Farm

Our farm is located in the heart of Green Valley, just 15 minutes from downtown. We welcome visitors for educational tours, class participation, and community events.

Directions

From Highway 101: Take Exit 45 (Green Valley Road) and head east for 3 miles. Turn right on Sustainable Farm Road. Our farm is located at the end of the road with ample parking available.

Visiting Hours

By appointment only

Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order for vegetables?

You can place orders through our contact form, by phone, or by visiting our market stand on Saturdays. We recommend ordering by Wednesday for weekend pickup or delivery.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery within a 15-mile radius of our farm for orders over $25. Delivery fees vary by distance and order size.

Can I visit the farm with my family?

Absolutely! We offer scheduled farm tours and family-friendly events throughout the year. Please contact us to arrange a visit.
